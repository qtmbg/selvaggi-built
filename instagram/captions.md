**Post 01 — We build around operations.**
Most contractors ask a facility to work around the build. We do the opposite. Selvaggi Built renovates active healthcare facilities across California — patients stay, medical teams keep working, schedules hold. Over the next 12 posts, we're showing you exactly how. Follow along.

**Post 02 — A hospital is a living system.**
A hospital never closes, never pauses, and never waits for construction. So we never ask it to. Every method we use exists for one reason: keep the facility fully operational while we build.

**Post 03 — The Zero-Disruption Protocol.**
Every Selvaggi Built renovation runs on one system: engineer the entire build sequence around facility operations — before a single tool is mobilized. Steps 1–3 coming up next.

**Post 04 — Step 1: Scan before we cut.**
Ground-penetrating radar. Critical utility mapping. Isolation zones established before mobilization. In an active hospital, surprises behind a wall aren't an inconvenience — they're a risk to patient care. We eliminate them up front.

**Post 05 — Step 2: Contain everything.**
ICRA Level 4 barriers. Rigid wall systems. HEPA-filtered negative air machines. Nothing crosses from the work zone into patient care — not dust, not noise, not pathogens.

**Post 06 — Step 3: Build on their clock.**
Disruptive tie-ins happen only in designated after-hours windows. Every phase is reviewed and approved by facility directors and Inspectors of Record before we proceed.

**Post 07 — Field standard.**
"The schedule serves the hospital. Not the build." It's not a slogan — it's how every Selvaggi Built project is sequenced from day one.

**Post 08 — Who we build for.**
Hospitals. Medical office. Behavioral health. Imaging and diagnostic. Surgery centers. Laboratory. If it's a healthcare environment that can't shut down, it's our environment.

**Post 09 — ICRA Level 4. Every project.**
The strictest infection-control tier in healthcare construction: full barriers, negative pressure, daily compliance logs. For us it's the default, not the upgrade.

**Post 10 — Licensed. Certified. Verified.**
HCAI project experience. ICRA-certified superintendents. CSLB License #1079406. Daily compliance documentation on every job. Credentials your facility's Infection Prevention team can verify.

**Post 11 — Built for continuity.**
The outcome of all of it: patients stay, medical teams keep working, schedules hold. We build the spaces where healing happens — without stopping the healing.
